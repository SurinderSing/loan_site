About Us

MyCredit India

At MyCredit India, we understand the need for money for those who are frantically moving from place to place to resolve an unwelcome financial crisis. We assist our customers/borrowers in obtaining financial assistance/loans from us with minimal techno-legal hiccups.

We have a simple and easy process for disbursing loans to lenders' accounts while submitting two major documents: UIDAI No (Aadhaar Number) and Bank Account Number.

We offer loan/financial assistance to any Indian citizen who has reached the age of 21 years, with amounts ranging from Rs.1 lakh to Rs.50 lakhs at a yearly interest rate of 12% only. The loan term ranges from 1 to 20 years and must be paid in equal instalments (EMI) that include the interest amount.
