FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

+
What is MyCredit India?

MyCredit India is a loan/credit line provider, serving as your one-stop shop.

+
When can I expect to receive my loan?

If all documents are in order, your loan should be approved within 24 hours. We work hard to ensure that you receive your loan as soon as possible.

+
What's the interest rate?

The interest rate is 1% per month.

+
What documentation is required?

The following documents are required to apply for MyCredit India. You must provide your PAN card, address proof, the most recent six months of bank statements, and three months of salary slips.

+
How much is the registration or applicable fee?

There is no registration fee.

+
Where can I apply for a Credit India loan?

You can get MyCredit India and use it wherever and whenever you want.

+
What is the minimum and maximum loan repayment period?

The minimum period is 1 year and maximum period is 20 years.

+
What is the loan APR on MyCredit India?

MyCredit India's loan APR is 12%, and the calculating formula is APR = [2 x Payments Per Year x Total Finance Charges] / [Original Loan Proceeds x Payments Per Year + 1]

Total loan cost and applicable fee example
Assume a one-year repayment period. The loan amount is Rs.10,000, the interest rate is 12% per year, and the loan term is one year. After one year, the total repayment amount is 10,000 + 1200 (12% ROI) = Rs 11,200.

Assume a ten-year repayment period
The loan amount is Rs.10,000, the interest rate is 12% per year, and the loan term is ten years. After ten years, the total repayment amount is 10,000 + 12000 (12% ROI x 10 years) = Rs.22000
